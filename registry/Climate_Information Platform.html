Climate Information Platform

Providing climate science basis with easy access to many pre-calculated climate indicators of weather and water in the past, present and future, for better planning of climate adaptation and mitigation activities.

Website: https://climateinformation.org/

Type of Digital Public Good

  • Open AI Model
  • ✅  Open Open Content: Source Code Repository
  • ✅  Open Open Data: Source Code Repository
  • Open Software
