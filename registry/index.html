Joining the DPG Registry

Once a nomination is submitted, it undergoes a technical review against the DPG Standard, a baseline of 9 indicators that serve as a minimum standard that all digital public goods must meet. Once the technical team reviews and approves that the documentation meets the standard, it gets added to the the DPGA registry.

For information on how to submit a nomination to the DPG Registry, see our submission guide.
