Please describe the circumstances with which this project shares data and/or content with third parties. Please add links as relevant.

Not Applicable

If yes - does the project ensure the privacy, security and integrity of this data and/or content collection and has it taken steps to prevent adverse impacts resulting from its collection, storage and distribution.

Yes

If yes - please describe the steps, and include a link to the privacy policy and/or terms of service:

Protections are four-fold:
- We avoid collecting many common forms of PII in the Kolibri Learning Platform, e.g. we don’t store email addresses or phone numbers, or birth dates (just the optional birth year).
- The demographic data (birth year and gender) fields are optional, and are not prerequisites for using the platform, allowing users for whom this information is more sensitive to opt out.
- Personal data that is stored in the offline platform is protected by industry standard authentication and role-based authorization techniques, and only made available to the user themselves or to someone authenticated as an administrator.
- When anonymized demographic data is reported from a device, it is reported in aggregate, in the form of the mean and standard deviation of the birth years, and the counts of users by gender. For devices with small numbers of users, these aggregate statistics are not calculated, to minimize identifiability.
