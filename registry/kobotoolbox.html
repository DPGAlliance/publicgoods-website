KoBoToolbox

KoBoToolbox is a suite of tools for field data collection for use in challenging environments

Website: https://www.kobotoolbox.org

Type of Digital Public Good

  • Open content
  • Open data
  • ✅  Open software: Source Code Repository
  • Open standard
  • Open AI model

1. Is it relevant to one of the Sustainable Development Goals?

  • 3. Good Health and Well-being

    Evidence: In Uganda, KoBoToolbox has been used to monitor deworming activities that are crucial to the health of school children

    Link to Evidence: https://blogs.cisco.com/csr/a-digital-data-collection-tool-for-humanitarians

  • 10. Reduced Inequality

    Evidence: In South Sudan, KoBoToolbox has been used to map remote health facilities and track available services and medicine

    Link to Evidence: https://blogs.cisco.com/csr/a-digital-data-collection-tool-for-humanitarians

  • 11. Sustainable Cities and Communities

    Evidence: In Niger, KoBoToolbox helps to plan and supervise infrastructure investments for refugees

    Link to Evidence: https://blogs.cisco.com/csr/a-digital-data-collection-tool-for-humanitarians

  • 16. Peace and Justice Strong Institutions

    Evidence: KoBoToolbox was selected as the official data collection and coordination tool for the Recovery and Peacebuilding Process in the Central African Republic

    Link to Evidence: https://blogs.cisco.com/csr/a-digital-data-collection-tool-for-humanitarians
