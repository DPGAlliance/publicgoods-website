Roadmap

Welcome to the Digital Public Goods Alliance Roadmap, a public visualisation of the key core, coordinated and aligned activities undertaken by DPGA organisations to advance digital public goods in a specific year. 

The Roadmap is a coordination, alignment, engagement and communication tool capturing stakeholder activities that significantly advance the four DPGA strategic objectives described in the DPGA 5 year strategy. 

Being part of the Roadmap represents an organisation’s commitment to advancing a shared vision, transparently monitoring and sharing their activities, and working collaboratively to support digital public goods. Explore the roadmap below to learn about the activities DPGA members are undertaking to help strengthen the DPG ecosystem.

Stakeholder organisations who participate in the annual Roadmap commit to:

  • Advancing a shared vision for digital public goods by carrying out activities that can significantly advance DPGA strategic objectives; 
  • Monitoring and sharing progress on DPG-related initiatives; and 
  • Driving international alignment around the DPG Standard. 

Roadmap participation is determined on a rolling annual-term, the Secretariat will invite stakeholders to add their activities to the 2021 Roadmap based on review and assessment of their activities. Organisations wishing to be included in the Roadmap can submit their activities for consideration through this form.
