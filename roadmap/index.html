Roadmap

The Digital Public Goods Alliance Roadmap is a tool designed to showcase activities that advance digital public goods and complement the DPGA’s strategic objectives.

Roadmap participation is determined on an annual basis, where the DPGA invites members to add their activities based on a review and assessment of their DPG-related work.

Explore the Roadmap below to learn about activities that help strengthen the DPG ecosystem. Each year, the DPGA Secretariat also publishes ‘The State of the DPG Ecosystem‘ report which provides further insight into the DPG-related activities that DPGA members have undertaken that year.
